Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2025

Last Updated: April 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Ticket Express ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our event management and ticketing platform ("Service"). This policy is designed to meet the requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

1. Who We Are

Ticket Express is a platform developed by Group 17 at Brock University. We act as the data controller for personal data you provide while using our platform.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

  • Identity Data: Full name, email address
  • Account Data: User role, login credentials (hashed), account preferences
  • Transaction Data: Ticket purchases, payment confirmations (no card data stored)
  • Event Data: Events attended, organized, or viewed
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device ID
  • Usage Data: Interaction with site features and pages
  • Communication Data: Emails sent, inquiries, support requests

3. Legal Bases for Processing

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent – for email notifications and optional services
  • Contract – to fulfill ticket purchases and registrations
  • Legal obligation – where required to comply with laws
  • Legitimate interests – for analytics, site security, and improving services

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To facilitate ticket purchases and event registration
  • To send email confirmations and reminders
  • To monitor platform usage and prevent fraud
  • To improve service functionality and performance

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including legal or academic obligations. Inactive or deleted accounts may be removed or anonymized after 12 months.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your personal data with:

  • Event organizers (only for events you register for)
  • Payment processors (e.g. Stripe, PayPal) for transactions
  • University stakeholders (for evaluation or academic oversight)
  • Authorities when legally required

We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third parties.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be stored or processed outside the EU or Canada. When this occurs, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

8. Your GDPR Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated data
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability in a machine-readable format
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

9. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and tracking tools to maintain sessions and analyze usage. You can control cookies via your browser settings. We do not use cookies for behavioral advertising.

10. Data Security

We apply encryption, access controls, and hashed password storage. However, no system is completely secure. Please use strong passwords and report any suspected breaches.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 16. If we learn that a child has provided us personal data without consent, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in law or service. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last Updated" date.
